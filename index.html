Help

Twitch - an online platform for live-streaming videos, primarily used for users to broadcast their video game gameplay videos and for others to be able to view these broadcasts.
Viewers - Individuals who are tuned in to watch the live-streaming videos, similar to Youtube or television viewers.
Channels/Streamers - The content creators on the Twitch platform. Individuals who stream/broadcast their gameplay videos for others to view. Streamers have channels, like tv channels. Their channel is categorized by the game that they are currently playing.
Players - All players that are playing the video games, not just those that are streaming on Twitch.

Attributes
Peak Viewer - The highest amount of concurrent viewers on Twitch for a certain game during a 24-hour period across all of its channels.
Average Viewer - The average amount of viewers on Twitch for a certain game in a 24-hour period across all of its channels.
Peak Channel - The highest amount of concurrent channels on Twitch for a certain game during a 24-hour period.
Peak Players - The highest amount of concurrent players in a certain game during a 24-hour period.
Game Rank - Twitch’s monthly ranking of the “Most Watched Games on Twitch”

Graphs
Heat Map - Allows you to see trends throughout the year between games.
Steaming Opportunity Line Graph- Allows you to see the average number of viewers per channel.
Star Plot - Allow you to compare game populations over one another.
Horizontal Bar Chart - Allows you to compare different attributes of multiple games and see the corresponding raw data.
